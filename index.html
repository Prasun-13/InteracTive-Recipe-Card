Chocolate Cake

Preparation Time: 45 minutes

Serving Size: 8 slices

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix dry ingredients.
  3. Add wet ingredients and mix well.
  4. Pour into a greased pan.
  5. Bake for 35–40 minutes.
